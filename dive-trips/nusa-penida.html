Nusa Penida

Manta Rays & Mola Mola, All Year Round

Overview

Nusa Penida is Bali's premier dive destination — famous for regular manta ray sightings all year round and seasonal encounters with the mola mola (oceanic sunfish). Expect vibrant coral reefs, unique marine life and thrilling drift dives around this island just off Bali's southeast coast.

Dive Sites

Manta Point

Shallow site (no need to go deeper than 12m) where reef manta rays — up to 5.5m wingspan — gather to feed and get cleaned.

Crystal Bay

One of the best spots in the world to see the oceanic sunfish (mola mola), typically from July to October.

Marine Life

Grey reef sharks Whitetip reef sharks Eagle rays Blue-spotted rays Lionfish Octopus Moray eels Scorpionfish Turtles

Recommended Level

Certified Open Water diver with 25+ logged dives and recent diving experience within the last 6 months.

Best Season

Mola mola: July–October. Manta rays: year-round.
