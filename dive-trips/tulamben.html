Dive Trip

Tulamben

Home to the USAT Liberty Shipwreck

Overview

About 2 hours from Sanur, Tulamben is a world-class dive site suited to every level, built around the USAT Liberty — a WWII cargo ship torpedoed in 1942 and pushed ashore by the 1963 eruption of Mount Agung, making it one of the world's most accessible wreck dives.

Dive Sites

USAT Liberty Wreck

125m long, 17m wide, resting at 6–40m. Shallow sections suit beginners; the deeper hull (35–40m) rewards experienced divers.

The Drop Off

A steep coral wall right next to the wreck, packed with reef fish and macro life.

Coral Garden

A shallow, easy-going reef ideal for check dives and photography.

Marine Life

Barracuda Turtles Hard & soft corals Pygmy seahorses Bumphead parrotfish ~400 reef fish species

Recommended Level

All levels welcome — shallow sections (~6m) for beginners, deep sections (35–40m) for experienced divers.

Water Temperature

26–30°C year-round.
