Amed

A Hidden Gem in Bali's North East

Overview

Amed is a hidden gem in Bali's north east, combining natural and artificial reefs. It's a favorite among macro photographers and muck diving enthusiasts alike.

Dive Sites

Jemeluk Wall

A beginner-friendly site with a steep wall to 40m, lined with coral and giant gorgonian fans.

The Pyramids

Pyramid-shaped artificial reefs at 12–25m, with a sandy bottom home to many blue-spotted rays.

Marine Life

Barracuda schools Japanese shipwreck Titan triggerfish Clownfish Trumpetfish Blue-spotted rays Nudibranchs Ghost pipefish Octopus & cuttlefish

Recommended Level

All levels — from beginners to experienced divers.

Best For

Macro photography and muck diving.
