Dive Trip

Night Dive / Safari

All-Inclusive Safari — 2 Days, 2 Locations, 6 Dives

Overview

A 2-day, 1-night all-inclusive safari-style adventure exploring Bali's north east coast — six dives across two of the island's best locations, including a memorable Tulamben night dive.

Locations

Tulamben

Centered on the USAT Liberty wreck (1942) — Bali's most popular dive site — plus The Drop Off and Coral Garden.

Amed

Stunning reefs with some of the most varied marine life, from tiny macro subjects to big pelagics.

Marine Life

Bumphead parrotfish Titan triggerfish Pipefish Blue-spotted rays Garden eels Nudibranchs Octopus & cuttlefish Sharks & turtles Barracuda

What to see during this night dive safari trip?

The historic USAT Liberty shipwreck and all its inhabitants such as: Bumphead parrot fish, Titan triggerfish, Damsel fish, Trumpet fish, blue spotted stingrays, garden eels, nudibranch, leaf scorpion fish, ghost pipefish, angel fish, octopus, cuttlefish… And lots of macro critters.

Things to know:

  • Diver experience level: Advanced open water (if you are still Open Water certified, ask us about including the Advanced course in this safari)
  • Trip: 2 days / 1 night
    Day 1: three dives Tulamben, overnight Tulamben
    Day 2: three dives Tulamben/Amed, overnight Sanur
  • Dive access: Shore access and local ‘jukung’ boat
  • Marine conditions: Muck diving, reef wall and wreck diving
  • Current: Low to moderate
  • Depth: 3m – 50m
  • Temperature: Typically 28 degrees or warmer
